CClaude is a privacy-focused AI assistant powered by Claude, designed with zero tracking and maximum user privacy in mind.

Key Features:
• 🤖 Chat with Claude AI - intelligent conversations without cloud storage
• 🔒 Privacy-first design - no tracking, no analytics, no telemetry
• 💾 Local memory - conversations stored locally on your device
• 🛡️ Security sandbox - multi-level approval system for AI actions
• 🌙 Open source - fully transparent, community-driven development
• 📱 Material Design 3 - modern, beautiful interface

Privacy Commitments:
• Zero tracking libraries
• No Google Play Services dependency
• No Firebase or proprietary analytics
• No internet permission for local features
• All data stays on your device

CClaude connects to Claude API directly, with no intermediary servers. Your API key is encrypted and stored securely on your device.
